About Me


Hello all, as you may know by now my name is Kimberly Padilla. I am currently enrolled as a junior in New Jersey Institute of Technology pursuing my bachelors in Information system. Prior to coming to NJIT, I went to Essex County College where I graduated with my associates in Information Systems. Thus far I have taken many classes and have used many programming languages along the way. Some the numerous programming languages I have used would be C++, Java, Python and more. However, out of all of the programming languages I've used, my favorite would have to be Python.
